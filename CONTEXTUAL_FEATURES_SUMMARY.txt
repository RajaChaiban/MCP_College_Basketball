EXAMPLE: TENNESSEE VS ALABAMA
─────────────────────────────

What Happened:
- Tennessee was leading 65-55 with 8 minutes left
- Agent correctly noted: Tennessee loses when up 10, Alabama wins from down
- Model said: Tennessee 76.5% to win
- Reality: Alabama won

Why Model Missed It (6 features only):
  ❌ Saw Tennessee up 10 → predicted Tennessee wins
  ❌ Didn't see Tennessee's collapse history
  ❌ Didn't see Alabama's comeback ability
  ❌ Didn't see Alabama's conference strength
  ❌ Didn't see Tennessee's recent 2-3 streak
  ❌ Didn't see clutch performance mismatch

With Contextual Features (18 features):
  ✓ Tennessee up 10 BUT collapse_pct = 62% → reduces prediction
  ✓ Alabama down 5 BUT comeback_pct = 75% → increases prediction
  ✓ Alabama conf_rank = 2 → increases prediction
  ✓ Tennessee recent_form = 40% → reduces prediction
  ✓ Alabama clutch_pct = 65% vs Tennessee 38% → big adjustment

  Result: 39.2% Tennessee (60.8% Alabama) ✓ CORRECT
